My Story

Turning curiosity into code

My name is Anzar Haider, and I grew up in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, India. From a young age I was curious about how computers work — and that curiosity never faded. It eventually led me to pursue a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science from Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, where I graduated in 2022.

After graduation I took a Java Full Stack Development course and sharpened my skills in both frontend and backend technologies. Alongside that, I gained valuable real-world experience working in logistics, operations and customer service — roles that taught me how businesses actually run, and why well-built software matters so much.

Today I combine my technical skills with that real-world business knowledge to build software that is not just beautiful, but genuinely useful. I am passionate about clean code, good user experience and continuous learning. My ambition is to grow into a senior software developer and contribute to impactful projects in India and internationally, particularly in the Gulf region.
